## Error Messages {#error-messages}

Error messages show up when the certain [Procedure Lines](../chapter_1_mobius_interface/procedure_line.md) fails to [execute](/chapter_1_mobius_interface/execute.md) because of bugs identified by Mobius. The error messages in Mobius include:

*   [Reference Error](Reference_Error.md)
*   [Type Error](Type_Error.md)
*   [Syntax Error](Syntax_Error.md)
*   [Error: Not an Entity](Not_an_Entity.md)
*   [Error: Not a List](Not_a_List.md)
*   [NaN](NaN.md)
